Meet Rooster

Paul “Rooster” Berler is a 20-year veteran of the music industry. He has taught every age group, played every musical genre, and led every type of musical group from afro-cuban jazz to zydeco.Through it all, his first love has always been teaching. He taught in the public schools for ten years and has been a private instructor for twenty. Currently he maintains an active schedule that includes performing, writing, critique, lecturing and of course, private lessons.
